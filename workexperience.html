Experience

Goodyear Napanee - Manufacturing Technology Co-Op

My 16-month QUIP placement was at The Goodyear Tire & Rubber Company in Napanee, Ontario.

Goodyear Napanee manufacturers tires for passenger and light trucks, producing approximately 20,000 tires per day.

There was a recent announcement of an expansion at the plant of more than $575 million CAD.

Part of the Quality Technology Team, my placement was as the Quality Specialist in the Curing Department. My responsibilities included recording and tracking non-conforming product, interpret data to resolve waste issues, explore solutions to reduce non-conformances, mitigate risks for team members and customers, and reduce costs.

I acted as project manager for the testing and implementation of a quality verification machine. The machine is used to verify the designs and text on the sidewall of the tire conform to DOT and legal requirements.
