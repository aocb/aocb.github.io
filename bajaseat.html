Baja SAE Bucket Seat

Designed for the Queen's Baja SAE Team, a carbon fibre bucket seat meant for use with a five-point harness.

Mass of 7.78 kg in a 58.0 x 45.0 x 61.6 cm volume.

Seat was designed for loads:

  • During tight cornering at high speeds (50 km/h, 2 m turning radius)
  • 1 m jump with a 70 kg driver
